About SAFE-NY

SAFE-NY is a comprehensive safety application designed for New York residents. With its intuitive interface, users can access detailed crime data to stay informed about neighborhood safety trends. Powered by a sophisticated database and regex system, SAFE-NY analyzes historical crime patterns to provide meaningful insights. The app also features a streamlined "panic button" that functions as a direct emergency contact tool, ensuring swift communication during critical situations. SAFE-NY empowers users with the information and tools they need to navigate their communities safely and confidently.

DISCLAIMER: This project was made by students for demonstrative and educational purposes only. We are not affiliated with NYPD or any government agency.
